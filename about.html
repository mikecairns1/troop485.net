About BSA Troop 485

Our Mission

BSA Troop 485 is dedicated to building character, citizenship, and personal fitness in the youth of San Antonio. We provide a supportive environment where young people can develop leadership skills, explore the outdoors, and make lifelong friendships.

Our troop emphasizes the Scout Oath and Law, encouraging scouts to be trustworthy, loyal, helpful, friendly, courteous, kind, obedient, cheerful, thrifty, brave, clean, and reverent.

Charter Organization

VFW Post 7108

We are proudly chartered by VFW Post 7108 in Helotes, Texas. The Veterans of Foreign Wars has been our steadfast partner in providing a meeting place and supporting our mission to develop young leaders.

The VFW's commitment to community service and youth development aligns perfectly with our scouting values, creating a strong foundation for our troop's activities and growth.

What Makes Us Special

Youth Leadership

Our troop is youth-led, with scouts taking on leadership roles and making decisions about troop activities. This approach develops confidence, responsibility, and leadership skills.

  • Senior Patrol Leader elected by scouts
  • Patrol Leaders guide their patrol members
  • Youth plan and lead meetings and activities
  • Regular leadership training opportunities

Outdoor Adventures

We believe in learning through outdoor experiences. Our scouts participate in monthly campouts, hiking trips, and summer camps that build character and outdoor skills.

  • Monthly campouts year-round
  • Summer camp at BSA camps
  • High adventure opportunities
  • Local hiking and outdoor activities

Community Service

Service to others is a cornerstone of scouting. Our troop regularly participates in community service projects that make a positive impact in San Antonio.

  • Food bank volunteering
  • Community clean-up projects
  • Supporting local charities
  • Eagle Scout service projects

Advancement Support

We provide comprehensive support for scout advancement, from Tenderfoot to Eagle Scout, with dedicated adult leaders and merit badge counselors.

  • Regular advancement opportunities
  • Merit badge counselors available
  • Eagle Scout project guidance
  • Boards of Review conducted monthly
