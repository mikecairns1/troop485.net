Interested in Joining?

We welcome new scouts and adult volunteers! Visit us during our regular troop meetings on Tuesday evenings to learn more about our program and meet our scouts and leaders.

For New Scouts

  • Must be at least 11 years old, or
  • Completed the 5th grade, or
  • Earned the Arrow of Light Award
  • Bring a parent or guardian to the first meeting

For Adult Volunteers

  • Complete Youth Protection Training
  • Fill out an adult application
  • Pass a background check
  • Meet with the troop committee
